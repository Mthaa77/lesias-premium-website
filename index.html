Choose the qualification that matches your future.

Lesia's offers a clear ECD career ladder: start with the Level 4 certificate, then grow into leadership through the Level 5 diploma.

Step 1 · Foundation

FET Certificate in Early Childhood Development

Start your formal ECD career with a recognised qualification designed for new entrants, daycare assistants, and passionate people who want to work with children professionally.

NQF Level 4SAQA ID 58761140 Credits12 MonthsSaturdays Only
  • Child development foundations and age-appropriate teaching methods.
  • Classroom management and early learning environment basics.
  • Preparation for assistant teacher, childcare worker, and daycare support roles.
  • A stepping stone toward the National Diploma.
Ask About Level 4
Step 2 · Advanced

National Diploma in Early Childhood Development

Advance into leadership with a qualification built for educators who want to teach independently, manage centres, design learning programmes, or open and grow their own daycare.

NQF Level 5SAQA ID 23118240 Credits18–24 MonthsSaturdays Only
  • Advanced teaching strategies and curriculum design.
  • Assessment methods, staff supervision, and centre leadership.
  • Business management, financial planning, marketing, and compliance.
  • Preparation for Grade R teaching, daycare ownership, and ECD management.

FAQ
Who should apply for Level 4?

Beginners, new entrants to ECD, aspiring daycare assistants, and students who want a recognised starting point.

Who should apply for Level 5?

Aspiring Grade R teachers, daycare owners, centre managers, and educators who want leadership and business skills.

Can I study while working?

Yes. The Saturday-only model is designed for working adults, parents, and people with weekday commitments.

Where are the campuses?

Soshanguve Block K at Khutso Primary School and a Hammanskraal campus in Gauteng.
